Closure & Exit — Customer Flows, redesigned against Closure OS v2.0

Every exit scenario in the Customer OS Closure journey, drawn as customer-facing screens. Each tab shows what the live app does today (where anything exists) beside the new Closure-OS screens. New screens are flagged. The principle: Closure finalizes custody; it does not judge departure — no silent exits, no lost devices, no money held with the device taken. Hindi-first, Wiom DS tokens, matched to the existing flow prototypes.

Grounded: Closure OS v2.0 (16 Jun) · ESR v2.3 · SPR v2.11 §6 · RR v2.3 · live app = wiom-tech/customer-app-kotlin

What this prototype covers

The exit zone is the least-specified part of the Customer OS today, and the most damaging when it fails. Ground truth from the field walkthrough (FS-02): 38.5% of pickup tickets are silent unrecovered devices · 27% of pickups happen but are never marked · 45.7% refund success rate · 524 orders stuck at "device taken, money held". These are large-scale current failures, not edge cases.

Closure OS v2.0 fixes them with two orthogonal state machines — relationship-side (what is owed) × custody-side (where the device is). They never collapse, so the system can never lose track of a device or a refund. CLOSURE_FINAL needs all five conditions: custody resolved · refund resolved (to Stage 3) · both bookend comms delivered · trust signal (if non-return) · obligations resolved.

Two journeys

4A — Self-initiated & reason-driven closure (8 tabs): the canonical machine. Nine of ten reason codes converge on CLOSURE_INITIATED and resolve to CLOSURE_FINAL, or reverse to ACTIVE_ENTITLED. SD-first recall, pro-rata auto-refund, the 4-stage refund tracker, zone-based reversal, custody dispute, bond write-off, dignified life-event closure, B2I pre-install closure.

4B — Wiom-initiated CSP migration (3 tabs): the critical reframing — a successful migration is CONNECTION_CHANGED continuity, not closure (same service_instance_id, no pickup, no refund). Only no-alternative and customer-declines branches reach Closure OS, with full refund and trust NONE — the customer did nothing wrong.
