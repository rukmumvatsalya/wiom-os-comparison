Wiom · Recharge / Activation — Case 2 of 2

Recharge Before Expiry → Queued Entitlement

The customer flow designed screen-by-screen from the B5 system sequence diagram, checked against the OS guardrails (Continuity OS · CM OS · CAEO/B2I · Closure OS). The plan is still active (FUNDED) when the customer recharges — so the new plan is queued, not blended. Both plans stay visible; at natural expiry (R0) the queued plan auto-activates at expiry + 1 second with no CAEO, no gap, and no "activating" wait (the internet was never off). If the customer exits while a plan is queued, the unused queued plan is fully refunded. Status/comms run in the in-app agent (chat); transactional steps are Design-System screens.
