Wiom · Customer Closure — Case 2 of 3

Customer Exit — Explicit Choice (Customer-Initiated)

The customer flow designed screen-by-screen from the B5 system sequence diagram, checked against the Closure OS guardrails. Here the customer actively decides to leave (taps Exit / self-raises a pickup). The system must honour it quickly, with no dark patterns, surface what they're owed (pro-rata + deposit) up front, offer self-return first, and record honestly — an exit on top of a Wiom failure is not laundered as happy churn. Comms (two bookends + refund) are multi-channel chat/notification; transactional steps are Design-System screens. The custody / refund / bond spine is shared with Case 1.
