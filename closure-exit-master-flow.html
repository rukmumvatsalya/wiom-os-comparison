The complete Closure & Exit flow — chat + DS screens

The whole closure journey as one connected flow, respecting the app's paradigm: comms, nudges & status live in chat (belief-stabilization — confirm state · clarify next action · reduce anxiety, with a pinned-context strip for the authoritative truth), and transactional steps are Wiom Design-System screens. Read top-to-bottom through 7 phases; lanes are the branches. Every surface is tagged chatChat or smartphoneScreen, plus Keep / Modify / Add new with its live Kotlin file.
