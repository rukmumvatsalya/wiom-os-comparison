Wiom · Recharge / Activation — Case 1 of 2

Plan Expires → Recharge → LIVE Activation (F1-SC-02)

The customer flow designed screen-by-screen from the B5 system sequence diagram, with every surface checked against the OS guardrails (Continuity OS · CAEO · SQ Driver · Recovery OS). The plan expired; the customer recharges on a LIVE connection. The system must communicate the invisible window between payment and internet restoration truthfully: pay → "Payment received" (not "active") → "Activating…" (ACTIVATION_PENDING) → "Internet is active" precisely when POST_ACTIVATION_VERIFIED (A13) fires — and start the entitlement clock at A13, never at payment. Status/comms run in the in-app agent (chat); transactional steps are Design-System screens.

Grounded: F1-SC-02 PRD (B5 sequence, B6 UI) · Continuity OS v2.0 §5.8 (AMD-CON-19, D-011) · CAEO · SQ Driver (CP-SQ-22) · Recovery OS · SPR v2.5
