Wiom · Customer Closure — Case 1 of 3

Customer Exit — Non-Reaffirmation (Passive Lapse)

The customer flow designed screen-by-screen from the B5 system sequence diagram, with every surface checked against the Closure OS guardrails. A customer simply stops recharging — no complaint, no declaration. The system must conclude the relationship without pressure, protect the ₹300 deposit, and never exit a customer whose silence was caused by a Wiom failure. Comms (recall + the two bookends) are multi-channel chat/notification; transactional steps are Design-System screens. Each surface is labelled with its B5 step and the guardrail it satisfies.
